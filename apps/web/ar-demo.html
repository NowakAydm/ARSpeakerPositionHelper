Best Practices for Speaker Positioning

  • Stereo Setup: Equal distance from each speaker to listening position
  • Triangle Formation: Form an equilateral triangle for optimal stereo imaging
  • Height Matching: Tweeters at ear level when seated
  • Room Acoustics: Consider wall reflections and room treatment
  • Near-field Monitors: Typically 3-8 feet from listening position

System Requirements

  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Device with camera (smartphone, tablet, laptop)
  • Camera permissions enabled
  • Good lighting conditions for best accuracy
  • HTTPS connection (required for camera access)
